SermonClips

What is a SermonClip

A SermonClip is custom motion graphic combined with clips of your speaker bringing home the Word. We have a proven process that helps us create high-quality video for you to share on social media. Each clip will be a 15 - 30 second highlight of a sermon with accompanying text so people can engage with your content regardless of their environment. You can even combine a SermonClip with your SeriesKit for a holistic theme across your platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a SermonClips package?

As we aim for a 2-3 day turnaround on SermonClips, we recommend ordering your package on Sunday afternoon or Monday morning if you're looking to get content up from this weekend.

Can I mix and match sermons?

Absolutely! A SermonClips package is for 4 clips. If you want 4 clips from the same sermon, or 1 of each from 4 different sermons, we can do that.

Can I make specific requests on what clips you use?

Of course! If there's a certain point in the message that you want us to highlight, send us the timecodes along with your order (don't worry, we already have a spot for it).

The sermon we're sending used SeriesKit graphics, is there any way the video can be themed to look like the rest of the graphics from this series?

As a SeriesKit customer, you can always request to have one or all of the videos in a SermonClips package themed to look like a current or past SeriesKit. Unfortunately, we can only do this with series designed by SeriesKit for licensing reasons.

Can a SermonClip use one of the worship songs we played on Sunday morning?

Generally we cannot do this for licensing and copyright reasons. We would hate to see a video removed from a platform after you paid money for it. There are special cases for songs that you have written and produced in-house, or for music you have licensed. In these special cases we recommend you add a note to your order and we'll follow up on the specifics.
