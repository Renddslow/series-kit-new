Frequently Asked Questions

What does a SeriesKit include?

A SeriesKit is anything and everything you need to display, post, present, or print to make your next sermon series amazing. This includes sermon slides (key points, Bible verses, illustrations), posters, flyers, t-shirt designs, social media templates, lower-thirds graphics and any announcement graphics you need for this series.

How does designing a series work?

We start with the vision of the series: what is the goal, theme, or main Scripture for the series. From there will rough up a 3-4 basic poster concepts that we think captures what you're going for. Once you pick a concept you like, we're off to the races.

When does the design process start?

We like to give at least a two week buffer between the start of the design process the first week of the series. This ensure that we have ample time to iron our all the kinks. But if you like to work further ahead, we're more than happy to be ahead of the game.

What are your business hours?

Our hours are 8am to 5pm Central Time. We are primarily located in Omaha, NE with teams in New York and Pennsylvania. We observe all federal United States holidays, but will honor a Saturday Night Special request up to the day of a major US holiday.

What if my church only does 8 or fewer sermons in a year?

We've thought about this one a lot. We like the flat fee because it lets us focus on solving your design challenges without having to fuss with different fees. As such we like to think about a single SeriesKit equating to about 4 sermons per month. So if you have a month where you need 6 sermons, but cancel the following month, we will prorate you for the remaining 2 weeks.

What is a Saturday Night Special?

A Saturday Night Special is any major design revision or creation needed for this Sunday that is received after 5pm Central Time on a Friday, or 5pm Central Time the day before a major US Holiday. We do provide 1-2 free vouchers for such a request (plan permitting), and do have rare but unfortunately needed exceptions for serious emergency or tragedy (though we hope you never have to use it). We will assess a $50 fee per use of this service. Read more about what qualifies as a Saturday Night Special.

What happens after I sign up?

You will receive an email with instructions on how to get the ball rolling with your first SeriesKit. As soon as we get your reply we will start working to mockup our 3-4 concepts.

How long does a design revision usually take?

While we tend to work fast, expect any major revision to have a 24 hour turn-around. However rather isolated minor cosmetic changes can be expected within a few hours depending on the volume of revisions at the time.

What qualifies as a Saturday Night Special?

  • Scripture verse for the sermon has completely changed and all scripture slides will need updated.
  • All main points have changed and note slides will need updated.

  • A guest speaker brought their own slides and they will need to be converted to our SeriesKit.
  • We have last minute changed the theme of the series/decided to do a one-off sermon.

    • Small quick tasks like a lower-third graphic needing a new name or updated falls within your unlimited revisions and will be completed at no charge.
